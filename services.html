Our Services

Care Tailored to Your Loved One's Unique Needs

At Grandnanny, we understand that choosing care for a loved one is deeply personal. Our services are designed to honor that trust — providing support that feels natural, dignified, and reassuring.

Support That Feels Like Family

Just as you once searched for the right caregiver for your children, you now seek someone who will treat your parents with that same level of compassion and respect.

Every care plan we create is as unique as the person receiving it — because no two individuals, and no two families, are the same.

Personalized care, delivered with heart.

Service

Personal Care

Delivered by certified caregivers, personal care is about more than physical assistance — it's about preserving dignity while providing the gentle, respectful support your loved one needs to feel comfortable and confident in their daily life.

Our caregivers approach every interaction with patience and warmth, ensuring that each person feels cared for — never rushed, never overlooked.

What's included:

  • Bathing and hygiene assistance
  • Dressing and grooming
  • Mobility and transfer support
  • Toileting assistance
  • Meal preparation
  • Medication reminders
  • Light housekeeping
  • Routine and schedule support

Service

Companionship

Loneliness can be one of the greatest challenges for seniors. Our companionship service is designed to bring meaningful connection, joy, and a sense of belonging back into your loved one's daily life.

Our caregivers aren't just present — they build real friendships. They listen, they share, and they genuinely care about the person they're with.

What's included:

  • Meaningful conversation
  • Games, hobbies, and activities
  • Reading and storytelling
  • Memory and reminiscence support
  • Emotional well-being check-ins
  • Light meal preparation together
  • Gentle exercise and movement
  • Companionship during meals

Service

Specialized Care

When your loved one requires more focused attention due to a medical condition or recovery process, our specialized care program provides the expertise and sensitivity the situation demands.

Our caregivers are trained to handle complex care needs with professionalism and grace — always prioritizing comfort, safety, and emotional well-being alongside physical care.

What's included:

  • Dementia and Alzheimer's care
  • Post-surgery recovery support
  • Chronic condition management
  • Palliative comfort care
  • Fall prevention strategies
  • Cognitive engagement activities
  • Health monitoring and reporting
  • Coordination with medical teams

Service

Respite Care

Caring for a loved one is an act of deep devotion — but even the most dedicated family caregivers need time to rest and recharge. Respite care ensures your loved one continues to receive exceptional care while you take the time you need.

Whether it's a few hours, a weekend, or an extended period, our caregivers step in seamlessly so you can step back with confidence.

What's included:

  • Temporary caregiver relief
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Same-quality care continuity
  • Overnight and weekend coverage
  • Emergency respite availability
  • Full personal care support
  • Activity and companionship
  • Detailed care updates for family
