About Grandnanny

We Believe Care Is One of the Most Important Decisions a Family Will Ever Make

At Grandnanny, this isn't just a service — it's a responsibility we carry with heart.

Who We Are

Honoring the Full Circle of Life

There was a time when you carefully chose someone to care for your children — someone you could trust, someone kind, someone who would treat them like their own.

Today, life has come full circle.

Now, it's your parents — the people who once cared for you — who need that same level of compassion, patience, and attention.

Grandnanny was created to honor that moment.

We provide premium in-home care for seniors across Ontario, focused not only on physical support, but on dignity, connection, and peace of mind.

Our Mission

Exceptional Care, Unwavering Commitment

At Grandnanny, our mission is to deliver exceptional, personalized in-home care that upholds the dignity, independence, and overall well-being of every individual we serve.

Guided by compassion and held to the highest professional standards, we are committed to providing families with complete peace of mind through certified Personal Support Workers (PSWs) and regular follow-ups — honoring the full circle of life by returning the same level of care, trust, and attention once given to us.

Through a foundation of professionalism, empathy, and reliability, we strive to create a seamless care experience that brings confidence, comfort, and reassurance to every family.

At the heart of everything we do is a deep commitment to supporting seniors with dignity and grace, while ensuring their loved ones feel secure knowing they are cared for with genuine compassion, respect, and unwavering attention.

Care that comes full circle.

Our Values

What Guides Everything We Do

Compassion & Companionship

Every interaction is guided by genuine empathy. Our PSWs build real friendships with the people they serve — because true care is felt, not just delivered.

Professionalism

We uphold the highest standards in everything we do. From PSW selection to care delivery with regular follow-ups, excellence is never compromised.

Dignity

We support seniors in maintaining their independence and sense of identity. Our goal is to empower — not just assist.

Reliability

Families count on us — and we take that seriously. With ongoing follow-ups and check-ins, we're here every step of the way to ensure your loved one's care stays exceptional.
